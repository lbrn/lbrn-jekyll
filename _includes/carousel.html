Welcome to the Louisiana Biomedical Research Network

The aim of LBRN is to enhance the quality of biomedical research conducted throughout the state of Louisiana by increasing access to state of the art research infrastructure.

LBRN Work In Progress Seminar Series

The Work In Progress Seminars are presentations by LBRN PI's where they present their research progress and have an opportunity for input from their peers and mentors in a collaborative setting.
