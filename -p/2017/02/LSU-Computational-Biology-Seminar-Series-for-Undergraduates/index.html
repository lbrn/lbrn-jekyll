LSU Computational Biology Seminar Series for Undergraduates

posted on 24 Feb 2017 by LBRN

March 29, 2017, LSU Life Sciences Auditorium Room 101 at 5pm-6pm, refreshments to follow.

Sponsored by the LSU College of Science, the Department of Biological Sciences, the Center for Computation & Technology and the Louisiana Biomedical Research Network

Speaker: Dr. Christopher M. Taylor

–Associate Professor - Microbiology, Immunology & Parasitology
–Director - Bioinformatics, Biostatistics & Computational Biology Core, Louisiana Biomedical Research Network

–LSU Health Sciences Center, New Orleans

Title: Visualization of Changes in the Vaginal Microbiome Over Time
